# Privacy Policy for DreamingSpanish Logger

## Data Collection
This extension collects:
- Your DreamingSpanish authentication token (stored locally on your device)
- YouTube video information (title, channel, URL) only when you manually click "Log to DS"

## Data Usage
- Authentication tokens are used only to communicate with the DreamingSpanish API
- Video information is sent to DreamingSpanish to log your language learning progress
- No data is collected automatically or in the background

## Data Storage
- Your authentication token is stored locally in your browser
- No data is stored on external servers by this extension

## Data Sharing
- Data is only shared with DreamingSpanish when you explicitly log a video
- No data is sold or shared with third parties
- No analytics or tracking services are used

## Data Deletion
- You can delete your stored token by uninstalling the extension
- To delete logged video data, use DreamingSpanish's account settings

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, please create an issue at: https://github.com/brianlund/ds-logger/issues
